Taxes and/or Fees
The Lowest Available Rate (LAR) you enter is used to derive room cost by deducting from it the contractual discount and, if applicable, per-night tax and/or fee components. Per-stay components are also deducted from the LAR if they are calculated at a % rate, but per-stay components set to a fixed-dollar amount and any per-person components cannot be deducted. Please see Help for details. Lowest Available Rate (LAR) shown here is calculated from cost, contractual discount and, if applicable, per-night taxes and/or fee components. Per-stay components are also included in the LAR amount if they are applied at a % rate, but per-stay components set to a fixed-dollar amount and per-person components are not included. Please see Help for details.
 Lowest Available Rate Help  
The Lowest Available Rate (LAR) cannot be applied over the specified date range because the deducted amount (contracted discount, taxes and/or fees) varies during this period. You will need to calculate the LAR separately for each of the date ranges below, or contact your market manager to adjust the deductions so that they are uniform across the desired date range.
